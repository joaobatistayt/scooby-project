Velma Dinkley

Velma Dace Dinkley, or simply Velma, is a character from the American animated series Scooby-Doo. Velma is intelligent, most of the time she is the one who solves the clues, puts the pieces of the puzzle together and explains the villain's plan step-by-step.

Shaggy Rogers

Norville "Shaggy" Rogers is a fictional character and one of the main characters in the Scooby-Doo franchise. He is generally depicted as cowardly, lazy, an amateur detective, and the long-time best friend of his cowardly dog, Scooby-Doo.

Scooby-Doo

Scoobert Cornelius Doo, better known as Scooby-Doo, is a fictional dog and the protagonist of the television series Scooby-Doo. Scooby-Doo is Shaggy Rogers' pet and lifelong companion. He can talk and stand on two legs for a long time.

Fred Jones

Fred Jones is a fictional character in the American animated series Scooby-Doo, leader of a quartet of mystery-solving teenagers and his companion Great Dane, Scooby-Doo. Fred has been portrayed primarily by voice actor Frank Welker since the character's inception in 1969.

Daphne Blake

Daphne Blake is a fictional character from the Scooby-Doo franchise. Daphne, portrayed as coming from a wealthy family, is known for her beauty, red hair, purple heels, fashion sense, and her talent for getting into danger, hence the nickname "Danger-Prone Daphne".
